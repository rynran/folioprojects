Your Digitized Closet

Combining AR technology and mobile body scanning to make finding an outfit easier than ever.

Outfitters Technologies aim to make the shopping experience easier for all consumers. With online shopping taking over the market, shoppers need more interactive experiences to feel confident about their purchases.

Using mobile body scanning, users take photos of themselves and create their own virtual avatar. Combined with AR technology, users are provided a virtual fitting room where they can make better choices about the clothes they’re buying online.
